About

Paladin Arms - Firearms Training

Welcome to Paladin Arms F.T. I am excited that you have chosen to take an interest in what I believe and the Constitution of the United States lays out, is an essential, God given right, to “keep and bear Arms”. Although there is a lot of responsibility that goes along with owning a firearm, there is also a lot of fun to be had as well! Not only for protection of yourself, family, and property, but also for hobby, sport, and collecting. Let me be one of the first to congratulate and greet you on your new journey in joining the 100 Million-plus gun owners in the U.S. and 5 Million first time gun owners in 2020 alone! I'm looking forward to help guide you through this new journey of firearms ownership! Welcome to the party...
